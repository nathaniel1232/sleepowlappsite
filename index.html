How it works

Three steps to better sleep

SleepOwl makes building a sleep routine feel effortless — and a little magical.

1

Set your sleep goal

Tell SleepOwl when you want to sleep and wake up. It builds a wind-down schedule that actually fits your life.

2

Complete nightly quests

Each evening, finish three small habits — breathe, journal, put the phone down. Earn dream stars for every quest you complete.

3

Watch your owl grow

Consistent sleep unlocks new growth stages and rare dream forms for your owl. The better you sleep, the more your companion thrives.
